Running a fuel station means juggling shifts, nozzle readings, cash drawers,
credit customers, suppliers, and a daily reconciliation that has to be exactly
right. Most software treats stations like a generic shop. PumpOS doesn't.

## Built around how stations actually work

- **Business-day & shift anchoring** — every record belongs to a business day;
  cash accountability rolls up per shift.
- **Nozzle-derived fuel sales** — sales come from opening/closing meter
  readings, not manual entry. Variance is a first-class, auditable number.
- **Immutable snapshots** — shift summaries and the Daily Station Sales Report
  are frozen at close, so reports never drift.
