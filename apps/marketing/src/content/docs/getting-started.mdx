Welcome to PumpOS. This guide gets your first station up and running.

## 1. Create your organization

Sign in and complete the onboarding wizard. You'll set your organization name,
legal details, and the first station's basics — timezone and business-day start
time (a fuel day commonly runs 06:00 → 06:00).

## 2. Model your forecourt

Add your **tanks**, **dispensers**, **nozzles**, and **products** with prices.
Nozzles map to tanks and products, which is how PumpOS derives fuel sales from
meter readings.

## 3. Open your first shift

From **Shifts**, open a shift, capture opening nozzle readings, and you're live.
