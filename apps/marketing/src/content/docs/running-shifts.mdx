---
title: Running shifts
description: Open, operate, and close a shift with accurate reconciliation.
order: 1
category: Operations
---

A shift is the operator-accountability window for the cash drawer.

## Opening

Capture opening nozzle readings (they default from the previous close). Record
the opening cash float.

## During the shift

- **Fuel sales** are derived from nozzle readings — no manual entry.
- Record **expenses**, **collections**, **purchases**, and **credit sales** as
  they happen. Only cash movements touch the drawer.

## Closing

Enter closing readings and count the drawer. PumpOS reconciles:

```
expectedDrawerCash =
  openingCash + cashSales + cashCollections
  − drawerExpenses − drawerSupplierPayments − cashDrops
```

Any difference is recorded as **variance** — a first-class, auditable number.
On close, an immutable **shift summary** snapshot is created.
